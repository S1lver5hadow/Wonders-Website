Explore an Ancient Pyramid

The Great Pyramid Of Giza can be found in Northern Egypt on a rocky plateau on the west bank of the Nile River. It was built in the 26th century as the tomb for the 4th dynasty Egyptian pharaoh Khufu. This tomb is the oldest wonder and what remains of the pyramid is only the underlying core structure.

Found in Giza, Egypt
Built in the 26th century
Took 27 years to build
Tomb of Pharaoh Khufu
Tallest man-made structure in the world for 3800 years
Originally had a height of 146.6m but is now 138.5m
Required 2.5 million stone blocks
UNESCO World Heritage site since 1979
