Explore a Marble Mausoleum

In 1632 the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan commissioned a tomb for his favourite wife Mumtaz Mahal on the southern bank of the river Yamuna, this led to the Taj Mahal being constructed. The Taj Mahal took 20 years to build at a cost of around $956 million and employed around 20,000 artisans under a board of architects. Now it is universally admired as a masterpiece of the world's heritage and a symbol of India's rich history, this attracts 8 million visitors every year.
